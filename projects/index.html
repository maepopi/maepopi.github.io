projects

Pipeline tools, XR experiences, games, and research-creation projects.

tools

Note: over the past few years I have developed extensive photogrammetry, 3D baking and optimization, LoRA/BLoRA training, and 3D-to-image workflows — but for confidentiality reasons, I cannot showcase them yet.

Audio Dataset Manager

An all-in-one tool for preparing audiobooks and large audio files for TTS training and voice cloning.

Diffusers in ComfyUI

A custom ComfyUI node that brings the full Hugging Face Diffusers pipeline into ComfyUI workflows.

research

Resilience

A virtual exhibition of a fictive artist, where each artwork reveals traces and echoes of her life.

Traces

A contemplative VR experience about Cultural Heritage, disappearance, and survival.

A contemplative VR experience sitting with Eleanor of Aquitaine.

Mémoires Gothiques

A VR exploration of gothic cathedral polychromy across two centuries.

A Unity application for the Centre Pompidou — 3D scans of objects from André Breton's studio, exhibited in the museum.

Dreamcatcher

A humorous VR simulator of the real Mona Lisa queue experience at the Louvre.
