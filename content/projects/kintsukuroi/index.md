*Kintsukuroi* is the Japanese art of repairing broken pottery by mending it with gold, to display the object's history rather than hiding it by fixing it back to its original state.

A spirit has hatched, but this ordeal put it in a bad state. Explore a beautiful and vibrant world, and feed on anything you can find to grow and heal.

Made for [Global Game Jam 2020](https://globalgamejam.org/2020/): *Repair*. Programming and character graphics by Charlotte. Environment by Maëlys Jusseaux and Camille Couturier. Music by Chad Crouch on Free Music Archive.
