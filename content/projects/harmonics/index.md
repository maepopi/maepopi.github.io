Harmonics is a contemplative game made in 48h for [Global Game Jam 2018](https://globalgamejam.org/2018/).

You play as a pair of forest spirits that can explore various places on a magical floating island, and awaken flowers by singing to them. Flowers may awaken other flowers in turn, and cause chain reactions.

Ideally the game is co-op: one player controls the movement with a joystick, and one controls the singing by playing bass at a microphone.
