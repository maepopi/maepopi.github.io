Help lost souls to find themselves, by aiding them in their personal struggles.

1. Talk to the ghost.
2. Pick up ingredients.
3. Make your potion in the cauldron.
4. Give the ghost its potion.
5. Repeat. There are 5 ghosts to help.

Made in 48h with [Charlotte RGB](https://glitchoris.itch.io) for [Global Game Jam 2021](https://globalgamejam.org/2021/).

**Tags:** Atmospheric · Casual · Emotional · Exploration · Fantasy · Female Protagonist · Relaxing · Short

**Controls:** Move — WASD / Arrow Keys / Left Joystick · Action — Spacebar / controller face button
